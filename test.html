How FreshSense Works

1. Place the Sensor

Install the FreshSense device in your mushroom growing room. It's about the size of your hand and requires no complicated setup.

2. Monitor Air Quality

The sensor continuously analyzes the air, looking for unusual odors that signal mold, bacteria, or contamination.

3. Receive Alerts

If something unusual is detected, you'll get an immediate alert on your phone with clear instructions.

4. Take Action

Address the issue early—adjust temperature, improve ventilation, or remove affected areas—before the problem spreads.
